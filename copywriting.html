Sety

Creator

Location: Philadelphia, PA

Years: 2018 - 2020

Stage: Beta

Tagline: Get out there

Sety (previously Datr.co) is in the business of making days. This near-term social scheduler flips inaction into a feature and connects users who like doing fun things three weeks at a time.

Product

Built with React Native, Sety is first and foremost a Social Scheduler: plan days up to five weeks in advance and connect within three. Got ghosted? Good! Sety’s up-to-the-day messaging system flips ghosting into a feature and, with the help of its two in-app monetizing features, connects fun people with interesting things on their time. Sety reached Beta spring 2020.

One of the more difficult aspects of the industry is effective monetization. Sety has two monetizing features "Add Days" and "Connects" and has an upcoming subscription framework ready for full product launch.

Branding

Sety is all about putting signals out into the universe three weeks at a time and is designed specifically to connect users with other rare breeds.
